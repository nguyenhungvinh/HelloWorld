Besides, Block chain is a part of database, where every data is stored in an public database, converted in the block form and they are tracked by multiple clients. These clients are connected each other, sharing the same data so that’s why it is called block chain. Even though block chain is a public database, it’s hardly to be attacked or harmed due to the way it protects the data. Once the data is published on the database, it is unchangeable. If there’s something needs to be considered to be updated, it must be verified and have all the agreements of the clients. Therefore, the data is likely unharmable by the hackers. Owing to that, there has been many industries apply this kind of database for the transaction, the most familiar example is Bitcoin, the transactions are performed under the control of the clients around the world so it is nearly can’t be hacked due to its complex system.

5. Project idea

    Overview

    As far as I have planned now, my project is mainly focus on creating a device which can give the user sense of reality even though they are in a virtualized world. This project is named Nerve Gear (NG), a helmet-like machine which allows players to merge their thoughts and brains into the device and transfer into the game. According to my plan, NG will be more advanced than the VR devices recently, which connect directly the user’s brain, neurons by microwave in order to “transfer” them into the virtualized world. This project is based on Sword Art Online novel, a well-known novel about RPG game where player can live their virtual life as the real one.

    Motivation

    The reason that makes me want to do this project is because I’m a fan of RPG game and love reading the Sword Art Online series, just like what I mentioned above. Since RPG games nowadays are all the rage, with a huge amount of them, and therefore, the players’ demand also increases. They want to experience something is virtual, however makes them feel real, just like they are actually living in the game, and I am no exception.

    Description

    As I have mentioned, NG will be a high-techno gaming device, much more advanced than VR machines in the future, where gamers not just they feel like they are living in the game, but it’s actually they are experiencing the reality in the virtual world. NG consists of high-density of microwave transceiver, which is capable of blocking the physical body movement sensory to avoid the players from moving and harming themselves while sending fake signal to their brain and spinal cord, making them completely insensate in the real life but live their physical life in the virtualized world. First of all, each user will have their own ID and data, merged with the NG device when they registered the account. Later then, the account and other related data will be sent to the client and stored on the main database. I am planning to create the database by applying the blockchain system due to its security. As I’ve mentioned, blockchain database can avoid 90% chance of being hacked, so it will be much safer for the users. After logging in to the device, the command from the main processor will be immediately sent to the transceiver to create the microwave and then, the movement command from the brain to the central nervous system will completely blocked and start controlling the user’s consciousness, while the fake sensory signal will be transferred into the brain and the spinal cord to make them feel they are physically interacting with the game, even though in reality they are not moving and remaining out cold. In addition, while experiencing the game, players can freely to adjust the physical aspects to make them feel much more “real”. To be more specific, they can set the painful level to make them feel like they just took the “real damage”, or even the temperature, … On the other hands, there will be an automatic switch installed into the NG, which is an emergency log out switch. Because the device will directly interact with the brain and the neuron system, therefore it can also detect whenever the user’s health is abnormal. If that happens, the safe log out command will be immediately executed, cut out all of the micro signal and return the user back into their reality. However, that’s not all the NG can do. NG’s main function is to create a physical simulator environment for human kind, thus it can be applied to numbers of areas, not only just for gaming. For instance, in army they can use it as a training system to improve the soldier’s shooting accuracy, simulate a battle field to make them familiar with war and train their reflex. It can come in handy for reducing the cost of producing weapons for the soldier’s training. Another positive example is applying for the medical field. NG will be the ideal way for the new doctors to train their surgery skill and they don’t have to interact with the death bodies.
